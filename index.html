Welcome to FundSelector

Mutual funds are one of the most important and popular investment ways for investors to maintain and increase the value of their assets. However, it is challenging for investors to select optimal mutual funds from thousands of fund choices managed by different managers. Investors often have their own personal preferences for mutual funds, and it is difficult to characterize different investors’ preferences quickly. Also, the mutual fund performance relies on multiple factors (e.g., the overall market trend and the investment style of fund managers), and most of these factors are dynamically changing, making it hard to compare different mutual funds in detail efficiently.

To address these challenges, we propose FundSelector, a visual analytics approach to facilitate mutual fund selection. Specifically, FundPicker incorporates a personalized ranking algorithm to enable personalized fund recommendation, and provides six well-coordinated visualization views to reveal the overall performances of recommended mutual funds from different perspectives and scales. Two novel visual designs are proposed to enable detailed comparisons of mutual funds. The augmented stacked bar chart provides interpretable fund ranking results by explicitly showing both positive and negative factors. The elastic trend chart allows investors to analyze and compare the temporal evolution of the selected funds’ performances in a customizable way. We conducted extensive evaluations of FundPicker, including three case studies, in-depth interviews with eight investors, and quantitative experiments for the personalized ranking algorithm. The results demonstrate the usefulness and effectiveness of FundPicker.

Data Resource

  • Stock Market Index: CSI 300 Index
  • Bond Market Index: Bond Market Index
  • Sectors Classification Standard: China Industry Classification Standard
  • Sector Indices: Chinese Sector Indices
  • Fund Data: Chinese Mutual Funds

Because fund data and sector indices are not allowed to be made public, we have made the source of all data public.

Special Note

The responsiveness of our demo is not fast enough. Our system supports calculating all funds' technical indicators in any period and the similarity of fund managers' investment styles. Thus our cloud server is not enough to compute such a large of data. Each service needs 10 seconds to 50 seconds on our cloud server. When our system is online, we will replace the cloud server with a better one, which will make our response time much shorter.

Data Service

The data service module, which is implemented based on the Flask backend framework and PyTorch, connects the database and the visualization module. To analyze and compare funds in a customized way, the system calculates technical indicators in real-time.

Visualization

The visualization module is implemented in the Vue.js frontend framework and D3.js. Investors can select and analyze funds through the visualization module.
